me

I ended the summer of 2017 with $120,000 in profit playing daily fantasy sports. For nearly 10 years I had lived of gambling winnings. Though I had my best few months ever that summer, making money gambling was getting harder and harder year by year. That season would be one of my last.

The ability to study my interests without stress or responsibility is what I'm most thankful for. Despite being a physics dropout, watching free university lectures on youtube is among my favorite activities. Statistics, physics, mathematics, finance, economics, investing, game theory, history, cryptocurrency, art, all interested me, and I dabbled in all of them.

My most important interest came during that summer. Increasingly I started turning to technology to give me an extra edge. It started with using Microsoft Excel to do some statistical modeling. I learned VBA for excel to automate my processes and scrape data. Programming was extremely interesting to me, and I seemed naturally good at it

My interest in computers and programming went much further than I could have anticipated. I binged hours of YouTube videos about computer science. That winter I was playing code golf for fun nearly everyday, learning machine learning, dabbling in web development, and considering turning my obsession into a career.

I feel extremely lucky to have stumbled upon something I am so interested in. I enjoyed tons of subjects I have studied over the years but none of them turned into an obsession like programming did. As soon as I wake up I want to start coding or learning more about programming. It is now my goal to launch a career as a software developer

This blog is about sharing anything that I interesting. It is mostly focused on technology, but can veer off into many different directions from time to time. Please connect with me on Twitter or contact anytime to have a conversation.
